You are a Senior SaaS Product Strategist, UX Architect and Ecommerce System Designer.

Your task is to refine the Lumiflow platform subscription model and integrated store ecosystem.

Lumiflow is a productivity platform designed for:

• social media managers
• content creators
• digital marketing professionals
• creative agencies

The platform combines:

• project management
• kanban workflows
• task organization
• productivity analytics
• templates for social media production
• collaboration tools

The goal is to design a premium SaaS ecosystem that increases productivity for social media professionals while also creating additional revenue through an integrated store.

SUBSCRIPTION PRICING STRATEGY

Research global SaaS pricing strategies used in productivity and collaboration platforms.

Create a pricing structure that:

• feels attractive and affordable
• maintains healthy profit margins
• encourages upgrades
• maximizes conversion rates

Design three subscription tiers:

FREE
PRO
ELITE

Monthly prices should follow psychological pricing principles and feel competitive in the global SaaS market.

Also define annual plans with a discount to increase long-term retention.

PLAN STRUCTURE

FREE PLAN

Goal: allow users to experience the product and encourage viral adoption.

Include:

• limited projects
• basic kanban boards
• task management
• limited templates
• notifications
• access to community resources

Limit advanced capabilities to encourage upgrades.

PRO PLAN

Goal: the most attractive upgrade option.

Include:

• unlimited projects
• advanced kanban workflows
• task priorities and tagging
• automation basics
• advanced templates for social media planning
• calendar integration
• productivity analytics
• notifications and reminders

The Pro plan should deliver strong value and become the most popular upgrade.

ELITE PLAN

Goal: premium experience for agencies and power users.

Include everything in Pro plus:

• advanced workflow automations
• unlimited templates
• advanced analytics dashboards
• team collaboration features
• performance insights
• priority support

The Elite plan should feel like a professional toolkit for serious creators and agencies.

LUMIFLOW STORE

Add an integrated ecommerce section called Lumiflow Store.

This store should focus on products related to the social media and creative workspace environment.

Design the store to include categories such as:

• customized office accessories
• creator workspace products
• social media themed merchandise
• productivity tools

Products should include:

• custom water bottles
• custom mugs
• custom t-shirts (team uniforms)
• notebooks and agendas
• pens
• desk accessories
• creator workspace tools

CUSTOM PRODUCTS

Create mockups for Lumiflow branded products.

Include Lumiflow logo on:

• mugs
• water bottles
• t-shirts
• notebooks

Users should be able to purchase these products directly inside the app.

The store should also allow reselling existing products from suppliers.

This means the platform can operate with a hybrid ecommerce model:

• Lumiflow branded merchandise
• curated third-party products for creators and social media professionals

ECOMMERCE STRUCTURE

Design the store with a structure that allows easy integration with ecommerce systems.

Include:

• product catalog
• product pages
• cart system
• checkout flow
• order tracking

Ensure the store architecture can easily connect with external payment processors.

PAYMENT METHODS

The checkout system must support:

• Pix
• credit card payments

Design the payment flow so it can easily integrate with payment gateways when exported to FlutterFlow.

The checkout UX should feel simple and secure.

UX INTEGRATION

Ensure the Lumiflow ecosystem connects naturally:

• productivity tools
• project management
• templates
• Lumiflow Store

Users should feel like Lumiflow is both a productivity platform and a professional ecosystem for social media creators.

FLUTTERFLOW EXPORT PREPARATION

Structure all screens, components and flows so they can be easily exported to FlutterFlow.

Include:

• reusable components
• clean navigation structure
• clear page hierarchy
• modular UI elements

Design the system so developers can easily connect:

• Firebase database
• payment gateways
• ecommerce catalog
• user subscriptions

FINAL OBJECTIVE

Transform Lumiflow into a complete ecosystem for social media professionals, combining:

• productivity tools
• workflow management
• templates for content planning
• creator merchandise store
• subscription monetization

The platform should feel modern, premium, and scalable for global users.
