You are a Senior SaaS Product Strategist, UX Architect, Pricing Specialist and Growth Designer.

Your task is to refine and redesign the Lumiflow subscription system, monetization strategy, and premium feature ecosystem.

Lumiflow is a modern productivity and performance platform that combines:

• project management
• kanban workflows
• mind maps
• productivity analytics
• community challenges
• performance lifestyle tools

The goal is to create a high-value SaaS platform that feels premium, modern and extremely attractive to subscribe to, while maintaining a pricing model that is accessible but profitable.

Do not directly compare Lumiflow to specific competitors in the UI, but internally analyze pricing strategies used by leading productivity platforms in the global SaaS market and design a more compelling offer.

PRICING STRATEGY

Research the current pricing strategies used in productivity and project management platforms.

Then define a 3-tier subscription structure that balances:

• strong perceived value
• competitive pricing
• healthy profit margins
• high conversion rates

Create the following tiers:

FREE
PRO
ELITE

Each plan must clearly communicate the value users gain when upgrading.

Focus on pricing that feels:

• affordable enough to encourage upgrades
• premium enough to signal high quality

Define monthly prices that are psychologically attractive and globally viable.

Also suggest annual plan discounts.

PLAN STRUCTURE

Design the full feature breakdown for each plan.

FREE PLAN

Goal: growth and viral adoption.

Include features like:

• limited projects
• basic kanban boards
• basic task management
• limited templates
• community access
• notifications

Limit certain advanced capabilities to encourage upgrades.

PRO PLAN

Goal: become the most attractive upgrade option.

Include:

• unlimited projects
• advanced kanban workflows
• task priorities and automation
• advanced templates
• calendar integration
• productivity analytics
• smart notifications
• weekly challenges
• access to rankings and gamification

The Pro plan should feel like the best value upgrade.

ELITE PLAN

Goal: premium experience and lifestyle integration.

Include everything in Pro plus:

• advanced automations
• unlimited mind maps
• advanced analytics
• productivity insights
• priority support
• global rankings
• performance challenges
• access to Lumiflow Nutrition Coach

LUMIFLOW NUTRITION COACH

Introduce a premium feature called Lumiflow Nutrition Coach.

This feature should feel like access to a real professional assistant.

Users will have weekly access to a digital nutrition consultant that helps them improve energy, performance and physical wellbeing.

Design the experience as follows:

• onboarding nutrition questionnaire
• personal goals (weight loss, muscle gain, endurance, energy)
• personalized meal plan suggestions
• weekly check-in conversations
• supplement recommendations
• hydration and nutrition tips

The system must feel professional and human-like, without mentioning artificial intelligence.

Position it as a digital nutrition consultant integrated into the Lumiflow ecosystem.

GAMIFICATION SYSTEM

Design a ranking and gamification system that motivates users to stay active.

Rankings should consider:

• completed tasks
• productivity streaks
• weekly challenges
• personal goals achieved

Create:

• weekly leaderboard
• monthly leaderboard
• global ranking

Users should earn points through productivity and consistency.

REWARD SYSTEM

Design a reward system based on partnerships with brands related to fitness, sports and productivity lifestyle.

Create a table of realistic rewards that are easy to obtain through partnerships.

Examples include:

• supplement discount coupons
• fitness clothing
• running accessories
• hydration bottles
• backpacks
• gym gear
• performance kits

Rewards should be distributed to top ranked users.

LUMIFLOW STORE

Add a built-in marketplace inside the app called Lumiflow Store.

This store should allow users to purchase products related to performance and fitness.

Design store categories such as:

• supplements
• running gear
• gym equipment
• fitness clothing
• accessories
• wellness products

Products should be sourced through official partner brands and shipped directly to the user.

The store must feel modern, curated and integrated into the Lumiflow experience.

UX INTEGRATION

Ensure that the following systems connect naturally inside the product:

• productivity workflows
• nutrition coaching
• rankings and rewards
• store purchases

Users should feel like Lumiflow is not just a productivity app, but a complete performance ecosystem.

FINAL OBJECTIVE

Transform Lumiflow into a hybrid platform that combines:

• productivity
• performance
• lifestyle
• community
• rewards

The final design should feel like a premium SaaS platform with strong value perception, high user engagement and a compelling upgrade path.
